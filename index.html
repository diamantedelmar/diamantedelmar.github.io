Welcome to Phaedra

Your stunning seaside retreat at Diamante del Mar, Kos

Explore Your Stay

Your Home Away From Home

Kalispera! We are thrilled to welcome you to Phaedra, a jewel within the Diamante del Mar complex. Named after the Cretan princess of Greek mythology, our apartment aims to offer you a stay filled with light, comfort, and unforgettable memories.

Phaedra is designed with a blend of modern amenities and traditional Aegean charm. Enjoy breathtaking sea views, spacious living areas, and everything you need for a perfect Greek island getaway.

We hope you'll feel right at home and enjoy exploring the beauty of Kos from this comfortable base.
